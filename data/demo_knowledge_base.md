## Frequently Asked Questions

### What services does Zenith Creative offer?
Zenith Creative offers four main service categories: Branding & Identity Design (brand refreshes and transformations), Website Design & Development (strategic, conversion-optimized websites), Design Services (print and digital design including presentations, collateral, and ads), and Marketing Strategy (from workshops to full-scale campaign planning).

### Who is the founder of Zenith Creative?
Sarah Mitchell is the Founder and Creative Director of Zenith Creative. She has a background in branding, UX design, and integrated marketing strategy, and has led projects for companies ranging from tech startups to Fortune 500 brands.

### What is the Elevate Framework?
The Elevate Framework is Zenith Creative's methodology for project success. It has three phases: DISCOVER (deep research into your brand and audience), DESIGN (crafting intentional visuals and messaging), and DELIVER (launching with precision and measuring results).

### What types of clients does Zenith Creative work with?
Zenith Creative works with Growth-Stage Startups, Small & Mid-Size Businesses, Enterprise Teams, and Agencies across industries including technology, professional services, e-commerce, healthcare, and nonprofit sectors.

### Does Zenith Creative offer free consultations?
Yes, Zenith Creative offers a free 30-minute strategy consultation. You can book one through our contact page at https://www.zenithcreative.demo/contact

### How can I contact Zenith Creative?
You can reach Zenith Creative by phone at 555.987.6543, email at hello@zenithcreative.demo, through our contact form, or via social media on Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

### What makes Zenith Creative different from other agencies?
Zenith Creative stands out through: a strategy-first approach grounded in research, flexible engagement models, a proven repeatable process, obsessive attention to detail, results-driven design, and a collaborative team genuinely invested in client success.

### What is the typical project timeline?
Timelines vary by scope: a logo project typically takes 3-4 weeks, a full brand identity 6-8 weeks, and a website project 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your free strategy consultation.
